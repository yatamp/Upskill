What Makes Up Your FICO Score
35%
Payment History
Single biggest factor. One 30-day late payment can drop your score 50–100 pts. Set autopay for minimums on every account.
30%
Credit Utilization
Keep total utilization under 30% — ideally under 10%. If you have a $10k limit, keep balance under $1k at statement close.
15%
Length of Credit History
Average age of all accounts. Never close your oldest card. A 2-year-old average age scores lower than a 7-year average.
10%
Credit Mix
Having both revolving (credit cards) and installment (auto/student loans) credit helps. Don't open accounts just for this.
10%
New Credit (Inquiries)
Hard inquiries stay on your report 2 years but only affect score ~1 year. Each new application can drop score 5–10 pts temporarily.
